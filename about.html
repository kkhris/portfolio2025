Hello Again :)

I think good design starts with paying attention — to people, to context, to the little things.

When things feel unclear, I tend to slow down, ask questions, and help bring some order to the chaos. I like to take things in before jumping to conclusions, which helps me spot what users need and what teams might overlook.

When I'm not designing, I'm walking on the seawall, dancing in empty gyms, recording vlog videos, and learning new languages.
