Hi! I'm Christal :)
From pixels to people, I design with innovation and heart.

I think good design starts with paying attention — to people, to context, to the little things.


When things feel unclear, I slow down, ask questions, and help bring some order to the chaos. I like to take things in before jumping to conclusions, which helps me spot what users need and what teams might miss.


When I'm not designing, I'm dancing in empty gyms, tripping over tennis balls, picking up golf, or filming vlogs I rarely edit. I’m always chasing new hobbies, sometimes successfully, sometimes chaotically, but always with curiosity.
